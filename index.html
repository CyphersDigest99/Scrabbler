How to Play

Your steampunk Scrabble word validator

Letter Wheel

  • Type letters to spin the drums
  • ← → move cursor between slots
  • ↑ ↓ cycle through letters
  • Enter validate your word
  • Backspace clear current slot
  • On mobile: swipe up/down to spin drums

Letter Rack

  • Enter your tiles (use ? for blanks)
  • Click Find Words to see all valid words
  • Use filters to narrow results

Quick Tips

  • Click the ☰ button for word lists
  • Click the 🎲 button for a random word
  • Click any validated word to see its definition
