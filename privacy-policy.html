Privacy Policy

Scrabbler - Steampunk Word Validator

Last updated: January 29, 2026

Overview

Scrabbler is a word validation tool that runs entirely in your browser. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data Collection

Scrabbler does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app runs locally on your device.

Network Requests

The only external network request the app makes is an optional word definition lookup via the Free Dictionary API (dictionaryapi.dev) when you tap a validated word. No personal information is sent with this request.

Local Storage

The app uses your browser's localStorage solely to remember whether you have completed the first-time guided tour. No personal information is stored.

Third-Party Services

Scrabbler does not use analytics, advertising, tracking, or any third-party services beyond the dictionary definition API mentioned above.

Children's Privacy

Scrabbler does not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL.

Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the GitHub repository.
